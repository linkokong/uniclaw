# Claw Universe — Logo Design Notes

## Overview

A clean, minimal, flat logo for **Claw Universe**, an AI agent/productivity universe concept. Built for instant recognition at any scale — favicon to billboard.

---

## Design Philosophy

**Name:** *Unified Orbit*
The mark communicates a product ecosystem orbiting a core intelligence — clean geometry, no clutter, zero ambiguity.

---

## Concept

**The "C + U" monogram as an orbital system.**

| Element | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Blue C-ring** (`#3B82F6`) | The "C" of Claw — an open circular arc suggesting orbit, motion, and openness |
| **Dark U-bar** (`#1F2937`) | The "U" of Universe — a solid U-shape anchoring the mark from below |
| **Three blue dots** | Claw toes / orbital satellites — three small dots echoing the paw concept |
| **Center blue dot** | The core — the AI agent at the center of the universe |
| **Dark bottom bar** | The U's solid base — grounding, reliable, structural |

---

## Colour Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Brand Blue | `#3B82F6` | Ring arc, 3 claw dots, center dot |
| Deep Slate | `#1F2937` | U-bar body (legs + bottom) |
| Background | Transparent | Works on any surface |

No gradients. No secondary colours. Two-purpose palette.

---

## Composition

```
        ╭───────────────╮   ← Blue C-ring (open on right)
        │               │
        │    ●          │   ← Center dot (universe core)
        │               │
   ╔════╧═══╗       ╔═══╧════╗   ← Dark U legs
   ║        ║       ║        ║
   ╚════╤═══╝       ╚═══╤════╝
   ─────┴─────────────────┘   ← Dark U bottom bar
      ●       ●       ●       ← 3 blue claw toes
```

- **Canvas:** 512 × 512 px, fully transparent background
- **Outer ring radius:** 206 px outer / 158 px inner (48 px stroke equivalent)
- **U leg height:** 115 px below center; 48 px thick bars
- **Dots:** 30 px diameter (claw toes), 34 px diameter (core)

---

## Why This Works

1. **Instant readability** — C and U are universally legible letterforms
2. **Two colours only** — maximum contrast, zero noise
3. **No gradients** — scales crisply from 16 px to billboard
4. **Centered composition** — balanced on all axes
5. **Meaningful forms** — ring = orbit/AI; U = universe/framework; dots = claw
